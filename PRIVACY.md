# Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

_Last updated: 2026-06-27_

## English

**Agent Port** is a local-first tool for monitoring and controlling agent
sessions (Claude Code, Codex, …) that run in `tmux` on your own computers.

### What we collect

**Nothing.** Agent Port does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal
data. The developer operates no servers and receives no analytics, telemetry, or
crash reports from the app.

### How your data is handled

- All connections are made **directly from your device to a service you run
  yourself** on your own Mac (over your LAN, Tailscale, or another network you
  control). Your session output, commands, screenshots, and tokens never pass
  through any server operated by the developer.
- Server connection profiles and access tokens you enter are stored **locally on
  your device** (in the app's local preferences) so the app can reconnect. They
  are not uploaded anywhere.
- The optional built-in **Demo mode** shows static sample data bundled with the
  app and makes no network requests.

### Third-party services

The app contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party
tracking. Any cloud features (e.g. text refinement or voice transcription) only
run if **you** configure them with **your own** credentials, in which case your
input is sent directly to the provider you chose, under that provider's privacy
policy.

## 简体中文

**Agent Port** 是一个本地优先(local-first)的工具,用于监控和控制在你自己电脑的
`tmux` 中运行的 agent 会话(Claude Code、Codex 等)。

### 我们收集什么

**不收集任何信息。** Agent Port 不收集、不上传、不出售、不分享任何个人数据。开发者
不运营任何服务器,也不会从 App 获取任何分析、遥测或崩溃报告。

### 数据如何处理

- 所有连接都是**从你的设备直接连接到你自己在 Mac 上运行的服务**(通过你自己控制的
  局域网、Tailscale 或其他网络)。你的会话输出、命令、截图和令牌不会经过开发者运营
  的任何服务器。
- 你填写的服务器连接配置和访问令牌**仅保存在你的设备本地**(App 的本地偏好设置中),
  用于重新连接,不会上传到任何地方。
- 内置的**演示(Demo)模式**仅展示随 App 打包的静态示例数据,不发起任何网络请求。

### 第三方服务

App 不含任何广告 SDK、统计分析 SDK 或第三方追踪。任何云端功能(如文本润色、语音转写)
仅在**你**使用**你自己**的凭证进行配置后才会运行;此时你的输入将直接发送到你所选择的
服务商,并适用该服务商的隐私政策。
